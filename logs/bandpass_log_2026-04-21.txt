WHY ELLIPTIC FILTER?
--------------------
- Steepest roll-off of any filter type
- Most efficient transition band
- Small ripples acceptable for neural signals
- Sharp cutoffs preserve HFO band (80-500 Hz)

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
--------------------
1. Used SOS (second-order sections) format for numerical stability
2. Applied sosfiltfilt for zero-phase filtering (no timing distortion)
3. Filter cached by sampling rate to avoid redesign
4. Frequencies normalized to Nyquist for digital filter design
